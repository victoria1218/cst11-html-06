What is Animal Crossing?

Crossing is a social simulation video game series developed and published by Nintendo.
The series was conceptualized and created by Katsuya Eguchi and Hisashi Nogami.Animal Crossing,the player character is a human who lives in a village inhabited by anthropomorphic animals and can do various activities like fishing, insect catching, and fossil hunting.
The series is notable for its open-ended gameplay and use of the video game console's internal clock and calendar to simulate real passage of time.
